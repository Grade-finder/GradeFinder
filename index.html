How to calculate your test score

1. Calculate the percentage:

The percentage score is calculated by dividing the number of correct answers by the total number of questions, then multiplying by 100.

2. Convert percentage to CGPA:

After calculating the percentage, convert it to CGPA using the following scale:

  • Percentage 90% and above = CGPA 10
  • Percentage 80% - 89.99% = CGPA 9
  • Percentage 70% - 79.99% = CGPA 8
  • Percentage 60% - 69.99% = CGPA 7
  • Percentage 50% - 59.99% = CGPA 6
  • Percentage 40% - 49.99% = CGPA 5
  • Below 40% = CGPA 4

This gives you both your percentage and CGPA based on your test performance.

How do I calculate my test grade?

a. Determine the total points and points earned.

b. Divide points earned by total points and multiply by 100 for the percentage.

Is 27 out of 40 a passing grade?

It depends on the grading scale. For a passing score of 60%, 27 out of 40 equals 67.5%, which is a passing grade. Check with your teacher for their specific scale.

What grade is 7 wrong out of 40?

7 wrong out of 40 equals 82.5%. Grading scales may vary, so confirm with your teacher.

Is 75 out of 80 an A?

Yes, 75 out of 80 is an A, which is 93.75% in the default grading scale.
